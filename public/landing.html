Coding Experience

The first time I learned programming was in high school, it was a choice between C and Pascal, I chose the latter one at the time. Although it was just elementary level of coding, I still found it fascinating to be able to automate stuffs in life with coding.

In recent years, I picked up coding again by picking a very beginner-friendly language to start with - Python. It is a very powerful language that has plenty of libraries and resources to use and I especially loved the ones that help monitor and auto-trade in the stocks and cryptocurrencies market.

Why do I enjoy coding?

I will write a very honest answer.

It's not just code:

Coding is not just typing some cryptic lines on the computer, it solves real world issues. I am able to forward this content to my fellows only because coding has empowered us well to communicate in an efficient way as human beings.

You can literally write code which can help people from the rural areas to order items from other countries, accept payments, connect, share & collaborate. Coding has a lot to offer.

Makes our lives easier:

Don’t want to drive in traffic, we have Uber.

Want to pre-book a villa, we have Airbnb.

Want to order food, we have plenty of apps for that as well.

and the list continues……………

Imagine if coding didn't exist, we wouldn't have any of these things.

Age is not the bar:

People at any age can begin to code, there is no restrictions. Coding empowers even a teenage kid who can now literally change not only his but our future as well.

I can literally keep writing and still I won't be able to even cover half the reasons why I love coding.
